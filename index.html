Presora — AI brand visibility monitoring tool

Presora is a SaaS platform for analyzing and monitoring brand visibility in generative AI models: ChatGPT (GPT-4o), Claude 3.5 (Anthropic), Gemini 1.5 Pro (Google), Perplexity, Mistral Large and Llama 3.1 (Meta).

The tool scores brands across 5 dimensions: authority, sentiment, accuracy, mention rate and recency. The AI visibility score is available in about 15 seconds.

Presora is used for: GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), AIO (AI Optimization), AI brand monitoring, competitor analysis in language models, and automated alerts on brand sentiment changes.
